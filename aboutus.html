ABOUT US

Founded in 1999, The Paarees Perfume. owns and operates over 100 Paarees Perfume and Designer Paarees stores. Our first store, Paarees Perfume, made its debut in November 1999 in Aptech learning, karachi.

Paarees Perfume is one of the nation’s largest retailers of genuine designer fragrances and related accessories. We also pride ourselves on being the leading authority on fragrances.
