Ck 2 by Calvin Klein Perfume. A new take on designer Calvin Klein’s initial successful fragrance, Ck, Ck2 is an updated take on the company’s most popular fragrance to date, and seeks to make it a perfect match for the modern perfume aficionado’s wardrobe. The top notes include pear, violet leaf, mandarin orange and hot wasabi, making it a unique creation right off the bat. The core of the fragrance is made of a combination of rose, orris root, pebbles, hedione and peony, while the base notes consist of white musk, vetiver, sandalwood and incense. Calvin Klein is an American design company that gained a huge amount of popularity in urban regions throughout the country during the 1990s. The brand was founded in 1960, and quickly became famous for the simple, minimalistic fashion styles it created for both men and women. After business was already booming from the company’s fashion designs, it began dabbling in perfume creation in 1981. Due to this initial success, Ck continued in the venture, and has since created 140 fragrances.
